I conducted parallel searches and targeted extractions to locate recent benchmarks, datasets, and analyses for evaluating tool-use safety of LLM agents between 2024–2026. Key sources located and reviewed include the Agent‑SafetyBench paper and codebase, the ToolSafety dataset and accompanying EMNLP paper, and an overview/curation of the Agentic Safety Benchmark (ATBench) concepts. I synthesized their scope, dataset sizes, metrics, failure‑mode taxonomies, evaluation methodologies, and reported quantitative outcomes to form a comprehensive research basis for a final report.
